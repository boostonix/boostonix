🌍 SEO Hacks for Local Businesses in 2025

By Boostonix • Smart SEO Guide

Want to dominate Google search in your city? Try these:

  • 1. Google Business Profile: Optimize every line, add photos, post weekly.
  • 2. Local Keywords: Use “dentist in Jaipur”, “best cafe near me”, etc.
  • 3. Reviews: Actively collect 5-star reviews and reply to them.
  • 4. Mobile Site Speed: Fast-loading site = better rankings
  • 5. Schema Markup: Use local business schema for better indexing.
