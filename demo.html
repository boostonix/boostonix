Boostonix Funnel Automation Demo - Real Estate

1. Lead Generation via Meta Ads

We launch highly targeted Meta (Facebook & Instagram) ads promoting a free resource, like:
• A property investment guide
• A price calculator
• An exclusive listing brochure

2. Lead Qualification via Form

Users fill a quick form: budget, location preference, phone number.
We instantly route and tag leads based on intent using Zapier or Make.com.

3. Instant Auto-Follow-Up

Once they submit the form, the system instantly:
- Sends a WhatsApp message with custom listings or agent intro
- Sends a branded email with next steps
- Sends a Slack/Email notification to your sales team

4. CRM + Sheets + Auto Tasks

We automate backend using your existing systems or our CRM. Lead is added to:
- Google Sheets (with timestamp)
- CRM (e.g. GoHighLevel, Zoho, Salesforce)
- Auto-tagged and sequenced for follow-ups

5. The Result

Speed wins. With this system, agents follow up in under 60 seconds, not 60 minutes. We’ve seen:
- 50% increase in hot lead conversions
- 30% more showing bookings
- 100% reduction in manual data entry
